Pin Mappings

General IO ports on the DroneLink motherboard can be specified in configuration files using their Port names (e.g. OUT0_0):

OUT0_0 32
OUT0_1 33
OUT1_0 14
OUT1_1 15
OUT2_0 2 (onboard LED)
OUT2_1 4
DAC0_0 25
DAC0_1 26
IN0_0 34 (input only)
IN0_1 35 (input only)

Serial port pins can be used for general IO by specifying their numeric values in configuration files:

Serial 0 (Tx / Rx) 1, 3
Serial 1 (Tx / Rx) 12, 13
Serial 2 (Tx / Rx) 17, 16
